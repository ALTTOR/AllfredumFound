Foundation

We are a non-profit NGO foundation, we started this dream two years ago helping patients who require medical help and medical supplements such as oxygen, insulin syringes and chemotherapy treatment.

The Allfredum Platform

Allfredum platform is to create a group of people who are permanent promoters of our cause, being our sponsors, since Allfredum Foundation has no income, it only receives donations to help those most in need, but nevertheless we require help to keep this page up and running. our server and thus continue helping as many people as possible.

Our current server is a machine with a capacity of 512 mb with apache installed, it is the one who gives service to this page, our main objective is to increase the memory and storage capacity. please help us to achieve it.

We are organizing a group of people who want to join our cause and thus be able to help more, and be able to offer faster help to requests.

I am a full-time programmer and this has allowed me to learn more about the ways to help through the internet, if you are a programmer, we could use a little help with our application.

I have received more requests from people who require help and require more storage capacity on my server, if you have a hard drive that you do not use, you will be welcome in my foundation.

Each donation helps the association to continue operating and offer help to more people, help us keep it running and avoid shutting it down due to lack of resources.

Each donation you make helps us to continue helping more people and this keeps us alive as an organization, continues to help, and remember that a small donation is always welcome.
